What is a Book Journal?

A Book Journal is a personal space where you can record your readings, reflections, and emotions about the books you read.

It’s a tool to organize your thoughts and track your progress as a reader in a personalized way, adapting to your needs if you want to keep a record of your readings.

A Book Journal can include reviews, favorite quotes, and notes about the characters and plots that impacted you the most.
